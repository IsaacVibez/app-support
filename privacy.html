Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Summary

Lawn Caddy is built for Florida homeowners who want to track lawn photos over time. We designed the app so routine lawn photo analysis runs on your iPhone. We do not use your photos to train cloud AI models or sell your images.

What we store on your device

  • Lawn photos you choose to add
  • Analysis results (color-based estimates, scores, recommendations)
  • Map locations for photo groups (front yard, side yard, etc.)
  • Profile details you enter (address, county, grass type, watering schedule)
  • Reminders you create

This data stays in the app’s local storage unless you delete it or remove the app.

Photos and analysis

When you add a photo, the app samples colors on your device to estimate turf condition. We do not upload photos to our servers for automated disease or pest diagnosis. Optional features (such as opening Apple Weather or Maps) use third-party apps under their own policies.

Location

If you enter a home address, we use geocoding to place your yard on a map and suggest Florida county watering rules. Coordinates and address fields are stored locally on your device.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. You can turn them off anytime in Settings or iOS Settings → Notifications.

Data export and deletion

You can export a text summary from Settings. “Delete All Data” removes photos, analyses, reminders, and profile information from this device.

Children

Lawn Caddy is intended for homeowners managing their own property. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
